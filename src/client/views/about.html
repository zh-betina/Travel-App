TripPrepper App

The TripPrepper App is a tool created in the learning process of the Front-End Development course from Udacity. It allows you to get general information about a given destination on a given date with possible hotel options and weather information. However, take into account that hotel pricing information might be not accurate and it is highly recommended to contact the chosen hotel stuff directly. The hotel information is to provide a general idea and propose some spots but by no means it replaces the actual room booking.

Stored in your browser memory...

The trips saved in the My Trips section are not stored in any database. If you clear your cache, your trip entries will disappear as it is the memory of your browser being used for saving and displaying those.

For instructions on how to use it, see Guide.
